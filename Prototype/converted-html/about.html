About Me


“Our cameras are the extensions of our eyes, and the light we choose is the heart of our images.”


With my starting salary after graduation I bought my first SLR camera. As I became more and more immersed in photography I started dreaming differently and decided to base decisions and goals on what is possible.

The most wonderful and mysterious aspect of photography is that every photograph is different. Unexpected, uncontrolled and even magical moments will unfold. With each photograph I try capture and accumulate those sweet realities of love and beautiful details that makes the story your own.

Me, my husband Riaan, and our golden retriever puppy Olive are based in Gauteng, South Africa. A real story of people past and present, of family and friends, and of honest love inspires me! I am a true romantic and I would love to meet with you over a cup of coffee or even on Skype and be part of your story.
